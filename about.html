About E&D Tech Solutions

A Nairobi-based team building digital products that matter.

We are a modern technology company rooted in Nairobi, Kenya — focused on helping businesses across East Africa grow through smart digital solutions.

Who We Are

E&D Tech Solutions is a Nairobi-based digital agency founded to bridge the gap between great ideas and great technology. We work with startups, SMEs, and established companies across Kenya to design and build digital products that perform.

Our team brings together expertise in web development, mobile app development, AI, cybersecurity, and IT consulting — all under one roof, right here in Nairobi.

What We Stand For

We believe Kenyan businesses deserve world-class technology without the world-class price tag. That means delivering polished, reliable digital products with strong foundations — products built to grow with your business.

Every project we take on is shaped by three things: creative direction, technical depth, and a clear focus on business outcomes.

Why Work With Us

We understand the Kenyan and East African market. We know what local businesses need — from M-Pesa integrations to multilingual interfaces — and we build with that context in mind. Our clients get a team that communicates clearly, delivers on time, and builds for the long term.

Based in Nairobi, we serve clients across Kenya and the wider East Africa region.
